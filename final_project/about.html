About me

Hello. I am Felix Levy and I am the creator of this website. I am a 15 year old french high schooler who currently lives in San Francisco. I am fairly new to the city since I have lived here for less than 2 years, so I wanted to build this website to let other people discover some places that I really like in San Francisco and that I recommend.

About my school

The French Highschool of San Francisco (its original name being Lycee Francais de San Francisco) is a french internation school in which students are taught in french but also in english in some classes.
