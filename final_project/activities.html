Activities

Visit the world-famous prison Alcatraz.

The prison of Alcatraz, located in the middle of the chilly waters of the bay, has housed some of America's most dangerous criminals, and was known as one of the most secured prisons in the world. It has now become a historic site, that is definitely worth a try. Get your tickets here.

Visit the Fisherman's Wharf and discover its variety of activities

Fisherman’s Wharf, is one of the city's busiest tourist areas. There are souvenir shops and food stalls at every turn, postcard views of the bay, Golden Gate and Alcatraz... There’s also a colony of sea lions to see and historic ships to tour, as well as Ghirardelli Square, where you can enjoy the iconic sundaes and some chocolate.

Enjoy a ride in the iconic cable car.

San Francisco's famous cable car is te world's last manually operated cable car system. This icon of the city will bring you from Union Square to the crest of Nob Hill, offering a thrilling way to visit the city.

A walk through the parks

San Francisco is full of green spaces : the famous Golden Gate Park, Dolores park, Presidio park... You can enjoy a walk through these beautiful spaces, visit museums, discover a lot of other attractions, or simply lay down and relax.

Seek out impressive murals in Mission district

The Mission District murals are a must see when visiting San Francisco. A lot of artists have left their mark on the walls of Mission District, making its streets look like outdoor galleries.
